# Biological Neurons

![[Pasted image 20250311105325.png]]

**Dendrite** : receives signals from other neurons
**Synapse** : point of connection to other neurons
**Soma** : processes the information (Central Processing Unit)
**Axon** : transmits the output of this neuron (Through which the output is carried on)

Humans have a massively parallel network of neurons of 10^11 neurons

![[Pasted image 20250311105714.png]]

The neurons in the brain are arranged in a hierarchy

![[Pasted image 20250311105851.png]]
